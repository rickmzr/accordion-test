Premium Performance

The new 2023 Toyota Sienna is versatile, powerful, and efficient, the perfect trifecta of performance features to carry you through whatever your day demands. With an innovative 2.5-liter 4-cylinder hybrid engine paired with an Electronically Controlled Variable Transmission (eCVT) powertrain, the new Toyota Sienna will deliver a class-leading 36 combined MPG. To optimize the capabilities of your vehicle, you can choose from a front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive system, both offering an athletic sport-tuned suspension.

In the new Sienna, you will also unlock unparalleled minivan performance, like a thrilling 245 combined horsepower, which aids in an impressive 3,500-pound towing capacity!
